About me

I'm based in Buenos Aires, Argentina. I have a degree in Hotel Management, but during the pandemic, I started studying web development because I wanted to take advantage of my free time and I was fascinated by the world of programming. I soon realized that it could be my next career. I started studying on my own and then took it seriously by enrolling my self in intensive course of frontend development.
